The Women Development Department is committed to the advancement and empowerment of women across Nigeria through various programs and initiatives.

Our Core Values

Service Excellence

Prompt and effective service delivery to women – constantly holding all women in high esteem to meet their needs.

Accountability

To the Nigerian women and the general public through delivery of service with loyalty, honesty, diligence and in transparent manners.

People-Centered Development

We believe in strategizing for the inclusion and socio-political upliftment of Nigerian women through advocacy, capacity building, and innovative initiatives.

Collaboration

We engage relevant organizations and partners to foster relationships and actualization of our vision, mission, and mandate.

Gender Equality

Promoting equal rights, responsibilities, and opportunities for all women and girls across Nigeria.

Innovation

Implementing transformative initiatives and best practices for women's empowerment and development.

Introduction

Advancing Women's Rights and Empowerment in Nigeria

Despite successive administration's efforts at integrating the woman's question into the development agenda, discrimination and inequalities against women remain pervasive within most Nigerian spaces. Hence, the focus of the women's department to ensure the promotion of the welfare and advancement of women for a sustainable development for the country through programme initiatives to discontinue the perpetration of socio-political injustice and underdevelopment.

In line with the mandate and vision of the Federal Ministry of Women Affairs, the Women Development Department is central to the attainment of the Ministry's mandate. In essence, it aims at drawing public attention to the fundamental rights of women, their potentials and the need for the society to guarantee women's equal access to development opportunities, eliminate discrimination in socio-political and economic life of women amongst several other areas.

Our Mandate

To advance the achievement of the global goal of equality, development and peace for women in Nigeria.

Our Vision

To develop project/programmes and advocate for a society that places premium on women's rights, empowerment, potentials and contributions to national development.

Our Mission

Ensure women's full protection, development and enjoyment of rights as an inalienable, integral and indivisible part of universal human rights for a sustainable development.

Key Responsibilities

  • Formulation and implementation of policies for women's development
  • Coordination of gender mainstreaming in national development
  • Empowerment programs for women and girls
  • Advocacy for women's rights and gender equality
  • Collaboration with stakeholders on women-focused initiatives

Programs and Initiatives

Vocational training programs in various trades to enhance women's economic independence and self-reliance.

  • Tailoring and fashion design
  • Cosmetology and beauty therapy
  • Catering and event management
  • ICT and digital skills training

Initiatives focused on improving women's health and overall wellbeing.

  • Maternal and child health programs
  • Reproductive health education
  • Breast and cervical cancer awareness
  • Mental health support services

Programs aimed at enhancing women's education and leadership potential.

  • Scholarships for girls' education
  • Leadership and mentorship programs
  • Entrepreneurship development
  • Political participation and governance
